About Cheney & Co

We realise that accounting, tax and business advice is different for every individual and business. We are committed to working with our clients to develop solutions, tailored to their specific needs. Our clients cover a broad range and we help them expand, develop and deal with problems and opportunities as and when they arise.

With four decades of experience, we offer a full range of accountancy and business services for businesses and individuals, which include audit and assurance, accounts preparation, preparation of tax returns, tax planning and advice for all UK taxes including Income tax, personal and business tax, capital gain tax, corporation tax and inheritance tax, trusts and estates, book-keeping, VAT advice and completion of VAT returns and payroll services. The list is endless.

Meet the Team

Paul Sparks

Partner

Having worked in the accounting profession for many years, I have found that the best way to help clients is to get to know them and to help them deal with their problems as they arise, rather than trying to sort the problem out once it has happened.

It is all a question of being proactive for clients, who in the small and medium size of business need all the help and guidance they can get, especially during these current financial times.

My expertise is in using the current tax rules and regulations to maximise profit extraction and minimise tax payments. The clients currently and previously looked after cover a large range of sectors and sizes which has enabled me to develop a vast amount of relevant business knowledge.

If you want to contact me then my telephone number is 01604 620106.

Specialties: Accountancy and tax advice for small and medium sized business, especially helping with new start ups and helping the owners of small business have someone to talk too and figure out what is good for each person with regards to the ever changing tax and accounting regulations.

Paul Cummings

Partner

Paul began his accountancy career with a Northampton based firm, before moving to a mid-size practice in Milton Keynes (now part of MHA) in 2012. After qualifying in 2018 he made the move over to Cheney & Co in 2019 becoming partner in 2020.

Paul has a large passion for systems and software, and had used his experience to assist clients to develop and grow their business.

Paul is a registered Responsible Individual for Audit purposes, as well as being the firm's Xero Champion and Quickbooks certified.

Paul is married with two young boys, and in his spare time enjoys watching sport and spending time with friends.
