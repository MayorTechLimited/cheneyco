About Cheney & Co

We realise that accounting, tax and business advice is different for every individual and business. We are committed to working with our clients to develop solutions, tailored to their specific needs. Our clients cover a broad range and we help them expand, develop and deal with problems and opportunities as and when they arise.

With four decades of experience, we offer a full range of accountancy and business services for businesses and individuals, which include audit and assurance, accounts preparation, preparation of tax returns, tax planning and advice for all UK taxes including Income tax, personal and business tax, capital gain tax, corporation tax and inheritance tax, trusts and estates, book-keeping, VAT advice and completion of VAT returns and payroll services. The list is endless.
